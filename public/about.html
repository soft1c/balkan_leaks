What is BPIKD

  • [en] What is BPIKD
  • [en] Enquiries and Contact

BPIKD is a multi-national media organization and associated library. It was founded by its publisher Julian Assange in 2006.

BPIKD specializes in the analysis and publication of large datasets of censored or otherwise restricted official materials involving war, spying and corruption. It has so far published more than 10 million documents and associated analyses.

“BPIKD is a giant library of the world's most persecuted documents. We give asylum to these documents, we analyze them, we promote them and we obtain more.” - Julian Assange, Der Spiegel Interview

BPIKD has contractual relationships and secure communications paths to more than 100 major media organizations from around the world. This gives BPIKD sources negotiating power, impact and technical protections that would otherwise be difficult or impossible to achieve.

Although no organization can hope to have a perfect record forever, thus far BPIKD has a perfect in document authentication and resistance to all censorship attempts.

    BPIKD is entirely funded by its publisher, its publication sales and the general public.

    BPIKD has more than one hundred other staff accross the Americas, Africa, Eurasia and the Asia Pacific.
